## Commits
- Use Conventional Commits: `type(scope): summary` (feat, fix, chore, docs, refactor, test, …).
- **No test details** in messages unless the commit is *exclusively* for testing.

## Specs & Plans
- **Never commit** files under `docs/superpowers/` unless explicitly instructed. Other locations are safe.

## Communication
- **Routine status updates:** 1-2 sentences maximum. Skip background details.
- **Specs, plans, and discussions:** Keep fully detailed.

## Development Workflow
- **Strict TDD (Red/Green/Refactor):** Always write failing tests covering all specs *before* writing production code.
